CHINESE ROBOTAXI STARTUP WERIDE CAN NOW CARRY PASSENGERS IN CARLIFORNIA
  • FIRMCADE
  • 2025
  • 4 Comments

Chinese startup WeRide has recieved approval from Carlifornia Public Utilities Commission (CPUC) to conduct passengers - carrying tests with its driverless vehicles. This three-year permit allows WeRide to transport passengers in both driver - operated and autonomous vehicles in San Jose and nearby areas.

However, under the permits terms, WeRide cannot offer rides to the general public or change for its services .Currently, WeRide operates 12 vehicles in Carlifornia , as confirmed by the CPUC. This moves marks a significant milestone for WeRide in the U.S autonomoud vehicle market , through the company might face additional scruitny due to its a Chinese background, especially amidst ongoing geopolitical tensions As WeRide proceeds with its testing , industry experts will be closely watching its perfomance , safety and ability to navigate U.S regulations.
