AIRLINK , XIAOMI TO ASSEMBLE SU7 ELECTRIC SPORTS CAR IN PAKISTAN
  • FIRMCADE
  • 2025
  • 2 Comments

Airlink Comminications ( AIRLINK ) demonstrated impressive financial growth, with earnings per share increasing from PKR 2.5 to PKR 11.7 year over year. As a leading player in the mobile phone market alongside its sudsidiary Select Technology , the company is now setting its sights on the electric vehicle ( EV ) industry.

The excitement surrounding this shift heightened with the arrival of the Xiaomi SU7 Max electric vehicle at Karachi Port, signaling Airlinks imminent entry into the EV market. The company is reportedly preparing to launch its own electric car Pakistan , which could signifantly impact both its future and the national economy.
