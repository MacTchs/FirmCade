Discover Peugeot 3008's blend of elegance and power.
  • FirmCade
  • 2025
  • 4 Comments

Discover Peugeot 3008's blend of elegance and power. The sleek Peugeot 3008 combines technology with elegance to display strength and character. The unrivalled Peugeot i-Cockpit® as well as the range of innovative technologies deployed on board have been designed to enhance your well-being and safety.

INSPIRED DESIGN
The exterior design of the Peugeot 3008 highlights its assertive character, balance of strength and smoothness. Its new breakthrough front end gives it a strong personality. This top-of-the-range model marks the beginning of a bold New era of modernity.
1.NEW FRONT END
The powerful, refined front end of the Peugeot 3008. A new frameless grille features at the front, while gloss black side scoops and a painted lower bumper trim frame the new design. NEW FRONT HEADLIGHTS Across the whole range, the front lightining signature includes LED headlights enhanced by the hook-shaped daytime running lights that are characteristically Peugeot and instantly recognizable, as well as full LED headlights featuring integrated Cornering Lamps.
2.18" LOS ANGELES ALLOT WHEELS
With wheel arch protectors and solid doorsils enhance the dynamic style of the Peugeot 3008.
3. NEW REAR LIGHTS
At the rear, the new Peugeot Full LED lights include a "3D lion claw" lightining signature and scrolling indicators. The lights have smoked glass lens. extending the black boot strip on either side of the vehicle and visually enlarging the rear of the car for an extra sporty look.
4. ALLURE
Crafted to exude confidence , the exterior design of the Peugeot 3OO8 SUV strikes a balance of power and suppleness. The striking front, defined by claw like LED signatures, encapsulates a harmonious interplay of power and refinement.
INNOVATIVE TECHNOLOGIES.
The latest-generation driving aids of the Peugeot 3008 further on-board comfort and safety.
1. ADVANCED GRIP CONTROL.
This is an advanced traction control system which allows the car to grip the road in all conditions by optimising the traction of both front wheels. Five modes are available : Standard, Snow , All Terrain , Sand and ESP.
2. HILL ASSIST DESCENT CONTROL (HADC).
This optimises vehicle control on steep slopes. When descending a slope with a gradient of more than 5% and with vehicle speed below 30 km/h the system will work to reduce the risk of slipping.During a descent the system maintains speed and brake control, allowing the driver to concentrate on the vehicle path of travel during the manoeuvre.
3. DRIVER ATTENTION WARNING.
A two stage driver alert system for added safety on the road, compromising: - A timed alert system operates at speeds in excess of 65km/h , after two hours of driving a message is displayed recommending that you take a break. - An active system that evaluates the vigilance of the driver at speeds of over 65 km/h.
4. ACTIVE LANE KEEPING ASSIST.
This is a system that detects, using a camera that recognises solid or broken lines, when longitudinal lane marking on the ground is accidentally crossed. It warns the driver and corrects the cars trajectory when the system identifies a risk.
5. SPEED LIMIT SIGN RECOGNITION ADVANCED RECOMMENDATION.
This system detects and reads speed limit and end of speed limit signs , and displays them in the instrument panel. The system also takes account of the speed limit information from the navigation system mapping. The system also features speed limit recommendation where the driver has the option to adapt their speed to the speed limit read by the camera via the cars, cruise control.
